Hey! I'm Ethan.

I'm a hardworking, energetic, and detail-oriented software engineering student passionate about developing for the future.

  I'm a second year student at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology in Oshawa, Ontario, studying Software Engineering. I started programming in the 4th grade, and have since worked on everything from websites to robots. In those years, I have self-taught over 15 languages with fluency, including javascript, C++, Python, and PHP. I have experience designing and developing functional software for websites, as well as robot and application code.

  Writing code is my art. I love learning and advancing my knowledge to remain at the forefront of software development. I'm always looking for opportunities to learn from others. My goal is to write code that leaves an impression on the user.

  If I'm not writing code, I can usually be found cycling, traveling, or playing music on my drums, piano, or ukulele. And of course, drinking coffee.
